Our Story

We are childhood friends that grew up together, but it wasn't until years later that we would end up dating. In fact, it was a 3 year process for us to get to this point in our story. Our love story started out long distance and it all began one day in July 2021 with a simple message. At first, we talked about the weather and everyday conversation. Eventually, our conversations started to deepen, and we started to learn more about each other. It wasn't until 2 years later when one of us admitted to the other that they had feelings, and it would be another 3 months before the first official date. The first date went well, and we decided to give dating a try. After a year of dating, we decided we wanted to get married. The one thing about our story is that it wasn't an instant connection nor was it love at first sight. It was due to a seed of love planted long ago and nurtured with friendship, acceptance, and understanding.
